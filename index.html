SERVER RULES

General Conduct

  • Respect all players and staff members at all times
  • No harassment, discrimination, or hate speech of any kind
  • English is the primary language in public channels
  • No spamming, flooding, or excessive caps in chat
  • Do not exploit bugs or glitches - report them instead

Roleplay Rules

  • Maintain character at all times - no meta-gaming
  • Value your character's life - no suicide unless roleplay dictates
  • Power-gaming and ninja-jacking are prohibited
  • Do not use third-party tools to gain advantages
  • Follow the New Life Rule - no returning to scenes of death

Cheating & Modifications

  • Absolute zero tolerance for cheating or hacks
  • No modded clients or unauthorized third-party software
  • Performance mods that give unfair advantages are forbidden
  • Texture packs and minor visual mods are permitted
  • Any suspicious activity will be investigated

Communication Policy

  • Use appropriate channels for their intended purposes
  • No advertising or promoting other servers
  • Do not share personal information of other players
  • Report issues through proper channels, not public chat
  • Listen to staff instructions - they have final authority

Punishment Guidelines

Violations will result in warnings, kicks, temporary bans, or permanent bans depending on severity. Repeat offenders face harsher penalties. Some violations may result in immediate permanent bans.
